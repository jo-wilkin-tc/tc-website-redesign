2017: a crisis year

California's worst heat year on record saw ED visits more than double the 2005 baseline, driven by prolonged summer heat across inland regions.

The inland divide

Imperial, Yuba, and Shasta consistently face rates 5–8× higher than coastal counties like San Francisco and San Mateo.

Who bears the burden

Black Californians face ED visit rates 2–3× higher than Asian/PI residents; adults 65+ are hospitalized at nearly twice the rate of working-age adults.
