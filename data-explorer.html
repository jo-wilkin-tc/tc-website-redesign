What the data shows

Reading this view

Statewide, annual average PM2.5 has trended down over the past decade, but the burden remains uneven. Communities in the San Joaquin Valley and parts of Los Angeles County still see levels above the statewide average, and those areas tend to report higher rates of childhood asthma emergency-department visits.

About this data

PM2.5 estimates combine regulatory monitor readings with modeled surface data; asthma measures come from emergency-department records. Definitions, sources, and caveats are in the about-the-data fact sheet.
